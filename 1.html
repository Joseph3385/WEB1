Trump attorneys pursuing voting fraud claims snatch defeat from jaws of victory

Three lawyers in the national spotlight for their
defense of President Trump scored major public victories before the Nov. 3 election, fueling conservatives hopes that they could overturn the loss to Joseph R. Biden.

But two months later, Sidney Powell, Lin Wood and Rudolph W. Giuliani failed in their mission.
